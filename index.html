Research Interest

1. Thermofluid Systems Optimization

Modeling and experimental study of efficient energy conversion in thermofluid systems, including solar collectors and heat exchangers.

2. Renewable and Sustainable Energy

Design and analysis of hybrid renewable systems (solar–wind) for off-grid applications in Ethiopia and Sub-Saharan Africa.

3. Refrigeration and Airconditioning

Numerical and experimental studies on the effect of phase change material.

4. Water desalination system

Numerical and experimental studies on the performance of desalination technologies.

5. Atmospheric water harvesting system

Numerical and experimental investigation of the performance of technologies based on the atmospheric condition.

Research and Technology Transfer projects

  • Design and manufacturing of solar thermal cooker.
  • Design and manufacturing of water desalination system.
  • Design and fabrication of vertical farming model for urban agriculture.
